Author Bio


Richard K. Ostrander is the author of The Epic of Hell Freeze and The MetaPhysician's Daughter
both published by BlazeVox Books. He has been published numerous times both in print and on-line.
Richard currently resides within the Carolinas and the interstitial spaces of thought and desire.
He has hosted numerous events, namely Java Expressions, the local open mic at the Coffee Scene
in Fayetteville, NC. Richard is active within his community, volunteering from time to time in the local
school systems, working with students on poetry and literature.

No more data is required other than the work herein which is more than mere biography.
Though some say it is about death, it is life. It is what stays the news!
